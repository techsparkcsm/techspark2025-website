About the Event

The Poster Presentation event provides a platform for students to showcase innovative ideas related to Artificial Intelligence. Topics may include AI applications, ethics, future trends, and real-world use cases.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. Participants must register for the event through online.
  2. Participation must be single only.
  3. Each participant must prepare a digital poster related to Artificial Intelligence.
  4. Posters must be original. Any plagiarism leads to disqualification.
  5. The poster must follow the specified format and be submitted in PDF format.
  6. Participants must carry and display their college ID card.
  7. Mobile phones are not permitted during the presentation session.
  8. Each participant will be given 15 minutes to present and explain their poster.
  9. Posters should be clear, visually appealing, and readable on a digital screen.
  10. Judgement will be based on content relevance, creativity, clarity, originality, and presentation skills.
  11. The decision of the judging panel will be final.
  12. Registration amount per person is 100 rupees.
