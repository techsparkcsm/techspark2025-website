SRK TECHSPARK 2025 | Department of CSE – AI & ML

About the Event

Paper Presentation provides a platform for students to present innovative research ideas, technical solutions, and emerging trends in the field of Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. Each participant can present individually or as a team (maximum 2 members).
  2. The paper must be original and unpublished.
  3. Paper topics should be related to CSE, AI, ML, or emerging technologies.
  4. Presentation time: 8 minutes + 2 minutes Q&A.
  5. PPT presentation is mandatory.
  6. Participants must submit the abstract before the event.
  7. Plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification.
  8. Judging criteria include innovation, technical content, clarity, and presentation.
  9. The judges’ decision will be final.
Amount per person: 100 Rupees
