Snap2Prompt

SRK TECHSPARK 2025 | Department of CSE – AI & ML

About the Event

Snap2Prompt is a creative and practical event where participants transform images into high-quality AI prompts. This event focuses on prompt engineering skills, clarity of instruction, and effective communication with generative AI systems.

Rules & Regulations

  1. Participation is individual only.
  2. A random image will be provided at the start of the event.
  3. Participants must analyze the image carefully.
  4. Create an effective AI prompt based on the given image.
  5. The prompt must be original, clear, and well-optimized.
  6. AI tools such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, or Midjourney/DALL·E may be used (as permitted).
  7. Internet access is allowed only for using AI tools.
  8. Pre-written or copied prompts/outputs are strictly prohibited.
  9. Submissions must include the image, final prompt, and generated output.
  10. Winners are decided based on prompt quality, creativity, relevance, and explanation.
  11. Registration amount per person is 50 rupees.
