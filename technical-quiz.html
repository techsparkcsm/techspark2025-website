Technical Quiz

SRK TECHSPARK 2025 | Department of CSE – AI & ML

About the Event

The Technical Quiz tests participants’ knowledge in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Science, and emerging technologies. It challenges students through time-bound multiple-choice questions, focusing on speed, accuracy, and conceptual clarity.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. Participants can register for the quiz online or spot registration is also available.
  2. The quiz time limit is 20 minutes.
  3. Registered participants must wear their college ID card during the competition.
  4. Participants are not allowed to switch tabs/apps during the quiz.
  5. Mobile phones are not permitted during the competition.
  6. The quiz will begin exactly at the scheduled time.
  7. The quiz will contain multiple-choice questions.
  8. Once the time is over, answers will be auto-submitted.
  9. Results will be generated based on the highest score and least time taken.
  10. Contact the student coordinator for further assistance.
Amount per person: 50 Rupees
