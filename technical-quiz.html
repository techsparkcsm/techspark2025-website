Technical Quiz

SRK TECHSPARK 2025 | Department of CSE – AI & ML

About the Event

The Technical Quiz tests participants’ knowledge in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Science, and emerging technologies. It challenges students through time-bound multiple-choice questions, focusing on speed, accuracy, and conceptual clarity.

Rules & Guidelines

  1. Participants can register online or through spot registration.
  2. The quiz duration is 20 minutes.
  3. Participants must wear their college ID card.
  4. Switching tabs or using mobile phones is prohibited.
  5. The quiz will start exactly at the scheduled time.
  6. All questions will be multiple-choice.
  7. Answers will be auto-submitted after time completion.
  8. Results are based on highest score and least time taken.
  9. Follow instructions given by the coordinators.
  10. Registration amount per person is 50 rupees.
